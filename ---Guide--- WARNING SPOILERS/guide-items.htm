Other Items:

Name Price Description Special Uses Other Notes
Embrosia Box N/A A strange box with a strange Q shaped imprint.... Used to defeat Quake one way in Chapter 1. Also is important to the Extended Mode-only parts. After Quake is defeated by this, it becomes an Accessory to equip on Tara. You must challenge Spark Mandrill (use Tara, Plo, Bonnie, [4th character]) and defeat him to get it.
Cross Key N/A A Key To A Locked Door... Used to unlock the Cross Door in Shademan's Temple[the right door]. The key is broken into a varying number of pieces, the amount of pieces is determined by the difficulty level.
Garlic Key N/A A Key To A Locked Door... Used to unlock the Garlic Door in Shademan's Temple[the left door]. The key can be found when you meet Dusty in the underground mines below Shademan's Temple. Defeat the Pizza Beast to get the key from Dusty.
Bat Key N/A A Key To A Locked Door... Used to unlock the Bat Door in Shademan's Temple[the center door]. You must challenge and attempt to defeat CacoVirgil to reach the switch that removes the statues blocking you from the chest containing this key.
Castor Key N/A A Key To A Locked Door... Used to unlock the Castor Door in Geminiman's hidden cave. Found by searching the caves in Geminiman's hidden cave.
Pullox Key N/A A Key To A Locked Door... Used to unlock the Pullox Door in Geminiman's hidden cave. Found by searching the caves in Geminiman's hidden cave.
Book of Hexen N/A Taken from the Sunken Castle 1st Floor It will help you understand Don's Quietus is necessary to remove the sword in the Janepo Ninja Castle After draining all water on the 1st floor of the sunken castle, a pointer will show on a bookcase. Helen must be with you to actually read and obtain the book.
Magical Blade N/A Found in Castle's Dungeon. Source of the adventure Marcus will take you to the Dainjora colonies to find out the true reason they want the sword. The first Quietus is necessary to dispel the barrier protecting the sword.
Gift Box N/A It says "To: Alex, From: Vernon" Take it to Alex, and one of four outcomes will happen, including the possibility to get 300,000 coins, or a pie in Alex's face. Go to Vernon at Jeremiah Castle and buy anything at all from him once, and he'll thank you by giving this to you. He also tells you to give it to Alex.
Lab Passkey N/A The Passkey for Bulma's Special Lab Workplace Allows you to unlock the door to Bulma's special laboratory at Capsule Corporation. With Vegeta present, talk to the doctor at the Capsule Corporation entrance, and he'll give this to you.
Spaceship Plans N/A Bulma's design plans for her new spaceship. Allows Bulma to eventually construct the spaceship. The strange bat-like creature will steal this from Bulma. You have to chase him around West City and defeat him to get them back.
I. Tracer 10,000 CAPs (Casino money) [CANNOT be sold] Detects the Presence of Items Hidden on the Ground Items hidden on the ground will become transparent and visible, and you will also get to see the type of items that are lying on the ground. You must gain entry to the VIP Room #2 in the Casino, and then gain roughly 10,000 CAPs to buy it. Also, you can use the I. Tracer confirmation code at the start of the game(given to you along with the item itself) to get the item immediately.
Ionic Key N/A A key to a special chest. Opens the Ionic Chest inside the lower-right cave on the Sunken-Castle second floor, which contains an Ionic Disruptor. You must find the key resting on a tree stump somewhere in the Shadow Wood, near the edges of the poison-swamp. The I. Tracer can detect the key if you've got it on.
VIP Card #1 1,000 CAPs (Casino money) [CANNOT be sold] A card for the VIP Room #1 of the Casino Allows you to go into the VIP Room #1 in the Casino. Get 1,000 CAPs in the front room of the Casino to buy this from the officer on the right side of the room.
VIP Card #2 2,500 CAPs (Casino money) [CANNOT be sold] A card for the VIP Room #2 of the Casino Allows you to go into the VIP Room #2 in the Casino. Get 2,500 CAPs in the front room/VIP Room #1 and give it to the officer in the VIP Room #1 to get the card.
